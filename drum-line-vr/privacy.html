Privacy Policy for Drum Line VR

Last Updated: 11/9/2024 (Month/Date/Year)

Thank you for playing Drum Line VR! At Nebular Games, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: We may collect information you provide directly, such as your username, email address, and other contact details if you create an account or reach out to our support team.

Microphone Access: With your permission, Drum Line VR accesses your device’s microphone to enable voice chat during multiplayer gameplay. This audio data is processed in real-time to allow communication among players but is not stored or recorded by us after the session ends.

Game Data and Analytics: While playing Drum Line VR, we collect data on gameplay events, scores, and usage patterns to enhance the game experience and improve our services. This data is usually anonymized and aggregated.

Technical Information: We collect technical information automatically, such as your IP address, device type, operating system, VR hardware, and other system data. This helps us ensure compatibility and improve performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Game Functionality: To provide the core features of Drum Line VR, including personalized gameplay experiences, voice chat capabilities, and progression tracking.
  • Improvements and Analytics: To analyze gameplay data and improve game mechanics, graphics, and user experience.
  • Customer Support: To assist you when you contact us with questions or concerns.
  • Security and Compliance: To detect and prevent fraud or illegal activity and ensure compliance with applicable laws.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. However, we may share information in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who provide services to help us operate and improve the game, such as analytics and cloud hosting.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, or when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as needed to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. Gameplay data may be retained in an anonymized format for historical analytics.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict its use. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [contact email].

6. Security

We employ various security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access and disclosure. However, no system can guarantee complete security, and we encourage you to take personal steps to secure your account information.

7. Children’s Privacy

Drum Line VR is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it as quickly as possible.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes and update the "Last Updated" date above.
